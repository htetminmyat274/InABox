Microwave Salmon Fillets

Quick, tender, and full of flavor, our Microwave Salmon Fillets are the perfect solution for a healthy, gourmet meal in minutes.

Add to cart

Ingredients

  1. Salmon Fillets (for 2 servings)
    • 2 fillets of salmon (approximately 6 oz each)
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
    • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh parsley for garnish
  2. White Rice (for 2 servings)
    • 1 cup white rice
    • 2 cups water or broth (for added flavor)
    • Salt (optional)
  3. Green Bean & Chickpea Salad
    • 1 cup green beans (steamed or blanched)
    • ½ cup canned chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
    • ½ cup canned kidney beans (drained and rinsed)
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon lemon juice or vinegar
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Prepare the Rice

    • Rinse the white rice in cold water.
    • In a pot, bring 2 cups of water (or broth) to a boil.
    • Add the rice and a pinch of salt, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the water is absorbed. Set aside.

  2. Step 2: Cook the Salmon

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) if baking, or heat a skillet on medium-high heat if pan-searing.
    • In a small bowl, mix the minced garlic, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and olive oil.
    • If baking: Place the salmon fillets in a baking dish, drizzle the garlic-lemon mixture on top, and dot each fillet with a small piece of butter. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
    • If pan-searing: Heat olive oil in a skillet. Add the salmon fillets (skin-side down if applicable) and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until golden and cooked through. Drizzle with garlic-lemon sauce while cooking.

  3. Step 3: Make the Green Bean & Chickpea Salad

    • In a medium bowl, combine the green beans, chickpeas, and kidney beans.
    • Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice or vinegar.
    • Toss to coat and season with salt and pepper to taste.

  4. Step 4: Serve

    • Place a salmon fillet on a plate alongside a portion of rice and the green bean & chickpea salad.
    • Garnish the salmon with fresh parsley if desired.
